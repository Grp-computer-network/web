Central Library

The central library is the ‘Heart’ of our institution. Established with the inception of the college, it houses 16,484 books, journals, periodicals, and newspapers. It fosters an environment for academic and intellectual growth, research, and learning.

Vision

To provide comprehensive resources and services that support research, teaching, and lifelong learning.

Mission

To empower knowledge creation through acquisition, organization, and dissemination of resources with value-added services.

Goals & Objectives

  • Enhance resources to meet academic needs.
  • Develop a robust digital library infrastructure.
  • Collaborate with national and international institutions.
  • Promote a culture of lifelong learning.

Library Rules & Regulations

  • Student ID card is mandatory for access.
  • Maintain strict silence in the library.
  • Report any damaged books; violators will be fined.
  • Mobile phones are prohibited inside the library.
  • Food and drinks are strictly not allowed.
