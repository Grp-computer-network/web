Institutional Development Plan


The primary vision of our college is to obtain knowledge, foster the students in a positive way and develop the intellectual skills to become empowered thinkers. Our college has steadily focused ahead since inspection, adapting to the various upcoming changes in the world and also retaining our core values of service, integration and discipline. Skill Development Centre and Centre for Excellence are introduced for the future growth of the College, this initiative will defiantly lead the Institution to next level.


The mission of our college is to provide quality and need-based education to the students with facilities of higher education. As per the mission, we have introduced several full-time programs with new and in-demand specializations. The PG programs have been introduced to meet the curriculum needs of the students. Research centre for Commerce has been initiated and 10 Research Scholars have been enrolled to take up research and lead the institution to the next level.

TNC Milestones

Centre For Excellence

A Center of Excellence (CoE) is a centralized team or organizational structure that provides leadership, best practices, research, support, and training in a specific area of focus. CoEs are designed to drive the adoption of new methodologies, improve skills, and ensure that the organization remains at the forefront of its industry.

e-Governance

Thiruthangal Nadar College embraces digital transformation through E-Governance. Our online systems ensure efficient administration, transparent operations, and seamless communication between students, faculty, and management.

Placement Cell

Our dedicated Placement Cell works tirelessly to bridge the gap between students and industry. We offer career guidance, skill enhancement programs, and campus recruitment opportunities with top companies, ensuring students are prepared for a successful career.

Incubation

The Incubation Cell fosters innovation and entrepreneurship among students. We provide mentorship, resources, and a platform to transform ideas into viable business ventures, empowering students to become future leaders.

SDC

The SDC is the backbone of student activities, promoting leadership and collaboration. With representatives from various departments, the council organizes events, fosters extracurricular growth, and ensures every student has a voice in college development.
