Cultural Association of Thiruthangal Nadar College


“RAGAM THALAM PALLAVI” – The Cultural Association

The Cultural Association “RAGAM THALAM PALLAVI” was introduced in the academic year 2014-2015 with a prime vision “WE ARE HERE TO MOTIVATE”. This Association identifies the talents of the students and offers proper training through various professional trainers from the respective fields. We give importance to both Academic and NonAcademic events like Paper Presentation, Model Making, Quiz Events, Web Designing, and Creative Activities and also the On- Stage and Off- Stage Cultural Events. The students are encouraged to participate in various inter-collegiate events and Importance is given for nourishment of Tamil Tradition & Culture.The association serves to be a channel to drive the students towards the path of victory. Even the cultural association of TNC will conduct inter-departmental competitions in the name of “TNC ARIEL ARTS”. the College is proud in organizing a Mega Inter-Collegiate culturals in the name of “UKTI” Every three years.

Highlights of Cultural Events

  • Inter-college Music Fest
  • Dance Competitions
  • Drama and Theatre Performances
  • Art and Photography Exhibitions
